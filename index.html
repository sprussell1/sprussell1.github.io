               <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-center text-[#073B4C] mb-4">The "Stay Home" Rule: Key Reasons for Exclusion</h2>
               <p class="max-w-3xl mx-auto text-center text-gray-600 mb-8">Knowing when to keep your child home is key to preventing outbreaks. Here are the most common symptoms that mean it's time for a sick day. Always check your school's specific policy.</p>

                   <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md text-center">
                       <span class="text-5xl">🌡️</span>
                       <h3 class="text-lg font-bold text-[#073B4C] mt-3 mb-2">Fever (100.4°F / 38°C+)</h3>
                       <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">Child must be fever-free for **24 hours** without using fever-reducing medicine before returning.</p>
                   </div>
                   <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md text-center">
                       <span class="text-5xl">🤢</span>
                       <h3 class="text-lg font-bold text-[#073B4C] mt-3 mb-2">Vomiting or Diarrhea</h3>
                       <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">Child must be symptom-free for **48 hours** after the last episode. This is critical for stomach bugs like Norovirus.</p>
                   </div>
                   <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md text-center">
                       <span class="text-5xl">😮‍💨</span>
                       <h3 class="text-lg font-bold text-[#073B4C] mt-3 mb-2">Uncontrolled Cough</h3>
                       <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">A persistent, severe cough that disrupts class or is accompanied by difficulty breathing requires staying home.</p>
                   </div>
                   <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md text-center">
                       <span class="text-5xl">🔴</span>
                       <h3 class="text-lg font-bold text-[#073B4C] mt-3 mb-2">Unexplained Rash</h3>
                       <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">A new or spreading rash, especially with a fever, needs a doctor's evaluation before returning to school.</p>
                   </div>
                   <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md text-center">
                       <span class="text-5xl">🤒</span>
                       <h3 class="text-lg font-bold text-[#073B4C] mt-3 mb-2">Sore Throat with Fever</h3>
                       <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">Could be strep throat. Child can return **12-24 hours** after starting antibiotics and being fever-free.</p>
                   </div>
                   <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md text-center">
                       <span class="text-5xl">👀</span>
                       <h3 class="text-lg font-bold text-[#073B4C] mt-3 mb-2">Eye Discharge</h3>
                       <p class="text-sm text-gray-600">Thick mucus or pus from the eye (bacterial pink eye) requires staying home until **24 hours** after treatment begins.</p>
                   </div>
